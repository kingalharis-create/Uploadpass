USAJOBS Photo Upload Error — How to Fix It in 2 Minutes

Updated April 2026 · 3 min read

If you're applying for a federal government job and USAJOBS is rejecting your profile photo, you're not alone. It's one of the most searched problems on the platform and the fix is almost always the same — your photo doesn't meet the technical requirements.

USAJOBS Photo Requirements

USAJOBS requires your profile photo to meet all of the following:

  • Minimum size: 100 × 100 pixels
  • Maximum file size: 5 MB
  • Format: JPEG or PNG
  • The photo should show your face clearly

Most common cause of USAJOBS photo errors: The file is over 5 MB, or the photo is in HEIC format (the default format on iPhones). USAJOBS does not accept HEIC files even though they look like normal photos on your device.

Why Is USAJOBS So Strict About Photos?

USAJOBS is a US federal government platform, and like all government systems it applies automated validation to file uploads. The system checks file format, file size, and minimum dimensions before accepting a photo. If any check fails, the upload is rejected — often with a vague error message that doesn't tell you exactly what's wrong.

iPhone Users — Read This

iPhones save photos in HEIC format by default. HEIC is a newer format that produces smaller files than JPEG, but it is not accepted by most government platforms including USAJOBS. If you're uploading directly from your iPhone camera roll, this is almost certainly the reason your photo is being rejected.

The fix is simple: convert the photo to JPEG before uploading. UploadPass does this automatically — just upload your HEIC file and download a JPEG.

Still Getting an Error?

If your photo still fails after converting and resizing, try these additional checks:

  • Make sure the file name doesn't have special characters — use simple names like photo.jpg
  • Try a different browser — Chrome tends to work most reliably with USAJOBS
  • Clear your browser cache and try the upload again
  • Make sure your photo shows your face clearly with no filters or heavy editing
