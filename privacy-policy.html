Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026 · Operated by King Alharis

Overview

UploadPass is a free browser-based tool that resizes and compresses photos to meet government and employer specifications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. The short version: we don't collect it.

Your Photos

All photo processing happens entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, never transmitted over the internet, and never seen or stored by anyone. The moment you close or refresh the page, your photo is gone.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following: photos or images you upload, your name or email address, payment information, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Analytics

UploadPass may use basic, anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit the site and which features are used. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

If you use the Pro version, payment is processed by a third-party provider (such as Stripe or Gumroad). We never see or store your payment details. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Third-Party Services

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Children's Privacy

UploadPass is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at kingalharis@gmail.com.
